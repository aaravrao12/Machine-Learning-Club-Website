Aarav Rao - President & Founder

Hi, I'm Aarav Rao, the President and Founder of the Machine Learning (ML) Club. I started this club because of my deep passion for AI, computer science, and problem-solving. My goal is to create a space where students can collaborate, learn, and apply machine learning to real-world challenges.

Right now, I'm working on a Smart Bin—a project that integrates machine learning to automatically sort recyclables from non-recyclables while detecting contamination. By improving waste sorting, I hope to enhance recycling efficiency and reduce environmental impact.

Apart from ML, I enjoy participating in hackathons, science fairs, and competitive programming. I’m always looking for new ways to apply technology to solve meaningful problems, and I can’t wait to see where the ML Club takes us!
